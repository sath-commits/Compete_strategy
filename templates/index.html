About Read Between The Hires

Intelligence hidden in plain sight

Every time a company posts a job, they reveal something about where they are going.

A cluster of iOS engineers signals a mobile push.
A wave of ML infrastructure roles signals they are building in-house models.
Multiple roles mentioning the same internal team name signals that team is expanding fast.

Read Between The Hires reads those signals automatically. Enter any company name and get a structured view of what they are likely building — backed by evidence from their own job postings.

How to use it

Three steps to competitive clarity

1
Search any company
Type a company name. Read Between The Hires fetches their recent job postings from across the web — LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor and more.
2
Read the signals
AI extracts tools, metrics, team names and business goals from each posting. Hiring patterns are grouped into strategic domains with consultant-style insights.
3
Ask deeper questions
Use the chat panel to ask specific questions: "How are they approaching enterprise sales?" or "What does this mean for my product roadmap?"
