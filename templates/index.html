About Read Between The Hires

Intelligence hidden in plain sight

Companies reveal strategy in more places than hiring alone.

A cluster of iOS engineers can signal a mobile push.
Official release notes and newsroom posts can show what they have already launched or announced.
Investor materials, when available, can show what management says matters most this quarter.

Read Between The Hires combines those signals into one view. Enter any company name and get a structured read on what they are likely building, shipping, or prioritizing — backed by evidence from job data APIs, SEC filings, investor materials, official product updates, and official GitHub activity when available.

Guardrail: this project only uses job APIs, official SEC endpoints, and explicitly configured company-owned pages or official GitHub organizations. It does not crawl arbitrary third-party sites.

How to use it

Three steps to competitive clarity

1
Search any company
Type a company name. Read Between The Hires starts with hiring data, then pulls in official SEC, investor, newsroom, release-note, and GitHub signals when they are clearly available and allowed.
2
Read the signal mix
AI extracts tools, metrics, launches, team names, management priorities, and product direction from each source. Those patterns are grouped into strategic domains with source-backed insights.
3
Ask deeper questions
Use the chat panel to ask specific questions like "What did management emphasize most?" or "How do the hiring and product signals change the competitive picture?"
